Essentials for Tomorrow's IT Infrastructure

Welcome to IT Power & Protection Zone, where you will learn more about the importance of improving the efficiency of your server room’s physical infrastructure. In addition, you’ll find valuable advice on how to create a comprehensive strategy for your company’s power, racks, management security and cooling systems that will save you money, energy, time and office space.

As we continue to embrace new technologies and deal with the staggering amount of mission-critical data that we now store electronically, it is essential for companies to keep their data safe, manageable and within reach. Solution providers must ensure that they provide a standard for quality, innovation and support in order to offer reliable solutions for everything from power protection and cooling strategies to network securities and physical server room threats for desktop systems, server rooms and general facilities.

With this, it’s imperative that organizations have systems in place to avoid costly outages. Furthermore, it’s become essential that next-generation server rooms provide consolidation, standardization and virtualization to compete in today’s fiercely competitive business environment.
