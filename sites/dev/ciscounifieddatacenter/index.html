New Economics, Dynamic Agility

Design Your Data Center for the Future

Unified Data Center allows organizations to take maximum advantage of the innovations enabled by virtualization and cloud computing through an innovative approach to data center infrastructure design. Using an architecture that unifies and dynamically optimizes computing, storage, networking and management resources, Unified Data Center lets IT departments deliver dramatic advances in business agility while fundamentally changing the economics of the data center. Organizations can unify technology silos and applications with a virtualization-ready infrastructure that delivers a cost-effective and evolutionary transition to next-generation data centers. The infrastructure is also optimized for the delivery of IT as a service, particularly in Cloud environments.
