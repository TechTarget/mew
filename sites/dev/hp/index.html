The Real Value of a Converged Infrastructure

There’s been a lot of industry buzz about IT infrastructure convergence, but not enough straight talk about how it can benefit your organization in practical ways.  This site provides a variety of educational resources to  help you gain a better perspective on the value of a converged infrastructure. Explore this site to learn how to:

  • Build a virtualized storage, server, and network infrastructure with integrated management
  • Upgrade your equipment to improve customer service, increase productivity, and reduce costs
  • Empower your data center to control energy use
