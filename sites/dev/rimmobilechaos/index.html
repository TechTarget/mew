Embracing Mobile Chaos

We live in a world of mobile chaos, where companies are forced to manage a growing diversity of portable devices, ensure seamless access to enterprise apps and enforce unified security policies. BYOD is the new reality; business users expect the freedom to work wherever, whenever and from whatever device they like. If your systems aren’t up to task, mobile chaos ensues.

Sound familiar? If so, you’ve come to the right place for help. “Embracing Mobile Chaos” is all about regaining control over your mobile infrastructure. This site features a multitude of videos, downloads and resources to help you along the way. There are also opportunities to connect with your peers via Twitter directly on the site.
